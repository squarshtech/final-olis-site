Welcome Message from the Head of School

Welcome to Osagyefo Leadership International School (OLIS), where excellence meets purpose. At OLIS, we are committed to nurturing students who think critically, act compassionately, and lead courageously in a rapidly changing world.

Our dedicated team of educators provides a stimulating environment that balances academic rigor with creativity and character development. Together, we inspire our learners to embrace leadership, service, and innovation.

Our Values

The core values that guide OLIS in its quest for excellence are:
  1. 1 Leadership
  2. 2 Service
  3. 3 Responsibilities
  4. 4 Integrity

OLIS views all its employees, especially the teachers who have close contact with the learners as crucial in the passing on of values to these pupils. Thus, all employees of OLIS are to live the above values as they demonstrate exemplary conduct to the learners.

Our Mission

Osagyefo Leadership International School will promote and build leadership in its pupils. It will achieve this total development of its pupils through the creation of a stimulating environment through curriculum and child-centered methods of teaching, to enable confront unfamiliar and uncertainties with courage and resolve.
